# StyleDesk — 30-Day Instagram Content Calendar

A 30-day launch runway built to drive the founding-100 promo. Each day has a theme, a hook, a suggested format, and a caption angle. Execute 1/day or batch-shoot a week in one sitting.

---

## Strategy
- **Goal:** 100 founding signups by May 2026 launch
- **Mix:** 40% features / 60% everything else
- **Format rotation:** static (design system) · carousel · reel · story
- **Every feed-post caption closes with the offer line:**
  > **100 founding shops lock in 20% off — for life.**
  > **Link in bio. May 2026.**

## Execution tips

### Batching
- Sunday: write captions for the week (1 hour)
- Monday/Thursday: shoot reels (2–3 hours)
- Static posts: build 3–4 at once using `_template.svg`
- Stories: improv daily, 30 seconds each

### Format rules
- **Static posts** → use the locked design system in `DESIGN-SYSTEM.md`
- **Carousels** → first slide is the hook, last slide is the CTA
- **Reels** → 7–15 seconds, voiceover over b-roll of your landing page or a salon
- **Stories** → daily, 3–5 per day, include a link sticker to your founding seats page

### Caption structure (every feed post)
```
[unique opener — speaks to this day's theme, 1–2 lines]

[bridge — optional, 1 line]

100 founding shops lock in 20% off — for life.
Link in bio. May 2026.

#barberlife #nailtech #dmvbarber #dmvnailtech ...
```

### When to post
- **Best window:** 6–9 PM ET (evening scroll, DMV local)
- **Worst time:** midday work hours
- **Reel posts:** 11 AM–1 PM OR 7–10 PM

### What NOT to do
- ❌ Skip days. Momentum matters more than perfection. Post a "quickie" story if you can't make a feed post.
- ❌ Recycle the same caption. Always rewrite the opener.
- ❌ Ignore DMs. Reply to every single one in the first 48 hours of a post going up.
- ❌ Chase vanity metrics. Signups matter, followers don't.

### Alternate durations

**If 30 days is too much:** run the **first 14 days** only. That covers launch, core features, and the founding-offer push — the highest-leverage posts.

**If 30 days isn't enough:** extend into month 2 with:
- Customer testimonials (once you have them)
- Case studies with real numbers ("shop went from missing 8 calls/day to 0")
- Before/after dashboards
- Long-form carousels explaining industry trends
