Questions & Answers

Common questions

Before you sign up, here's what shop owners ask us most.

Can I keep my current phone number and still use the AI?

Yes — just forward your number to StyleDesk. Takes a minute, we walk you through it after signup. Your clients keep calling the same number they always have.

Do I need to be technical to set this up?

No. If you can send a text, you can run StyleDesk. Setup is point-and-click in your browser — type your services, pick an area code, hit save. Most shops are live within 15 minutes.

Can the AI transfer a call to me if someone really needs to talk?

Yes. Callers can press 1 anytime to reach you directly. If you don't pick up, they can leave a voicemail that appears in your dashboard — with an AI transcript on Pro and Premium plans so you can read it at a glance.

What happens during the free trial?

Basic gets 14 days. Pro and Premium get 30 days. Full access to everything — no charge until the trial ends. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with one click.

Will I get hit with extra charges if my shop gets busy?

No. Every plan is flat monthly — no per-minute fees, no per-text fees, no overages. Your $49, $179, or $299 covers everything.

Does the AI really understand what a shape-up or gel mani is?

Yes. StyleDesk knows 500+ shop terms out of the box — fades, tapers, skin fades, shape-ups, gel manis, dip powder, ombre, and hundreds more. No configuration needed.

Can the AI talk to clients in Spanish?

Yes. Pro lets you pick up to 5 languages from 30 supported (Spanish, French, Portuguese, and more). Premium unlocks all 30. Once enabled in your settings, the AI detects each caller's language and responds in it automatically — no prompting needed per call.
