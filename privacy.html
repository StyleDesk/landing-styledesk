Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

StyleDesk AI, LLC ("StyleDesk," "we," "us," or "our") operates the StyleDesk.ai platform, including our website, dashboards, AI receptionist service, and related tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using StyleDesk.ai, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business address (if provided)
  • Booking platform URL
  • Service menu, pricing, and business hours
  • Plan selection and subscription status

1.2 Call and Text Data

When our AI receptionist handles calls and texts on your behalf, we collect:

  • Caller and sender phone numbers
  • Call duration, timestamps, and status
  • Call recordings and transcriptions
  • Text message content and conversation history
  • Voicemail recordings and transcriptions
  • Topics discussed during calls (e.g., pricing, booking, hours)
  • Whether a booking link was sent

1.3 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, Inc. We do not store, process, or have access to your full credit card number, debit card number, or bank account details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data. We receive only a confirmation of payment status, last four digits of your card, and billing address from Stripe.

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our platform, including:

  • Pages visited and features used within the dashboard
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Referring website or source
  • Session duration and interaction patterns

1.5 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may limit some functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI receptionist service
  • Answer calls and texts on your behalf using your business information
  • Send booking links to your callers and texters automatically
  • Display call and text history in your dashboard
  • Generate reports, insights, and analytics for your business
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmations, receipts, reports)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Respond to your support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only with the following categories of service providers, strictly as needed to operate our platform:

3.1 Telecommunications

We use Twilio, Inc. to power our phone number provisioning, call handling, and SMS messaging. Twilio processes caller phone numbers, call audio, and text message content on our behalf. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data.

3.2 Artificial Intelligence

We use AI language models provided by OpenAI and/or Anthropic to generate conversational responses during calls and texts. Conversation content is sent to these providers for real-time processing. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models. These providers process data under strict data processing agreements.

3.3 Payment Processing

We use Stripe, Inc. to securely process all payments. Your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

3.4 Infrastructure and Hosting

We use trusted third-party cloud hosting, database, and content delivery providers to store and serve your data securely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

3.5 Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our website and platform. This data is anonymized and aggregated where possible.

3.6 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

4. Call Recording and AI Disclosure

Our AI receptionist may record calls for quality assurance and to provide call transcription features. When applicable:

  • Callers are informed at the beginning of the call that they are speaking with an AI assistant
  • Call recordings are stored securely and accessible only to the business owner through their dashboard
  • Recordings are retained in accordance with our data retention policy (see Section 8)
  • You are responsible for ensuring compliance with any state or local call recording laws that may apply to your jurisdiction

Some states require all-party consent for call recording. StyleDesk provides an AI disclosure at the start of each call, but you should consult with a legal professional to ensure compliance with your specific state and local laws.

5. SMS and Text Messaging

  • When a customer texts your StyleDesk business number, they consent to receiving AI-generated replies related to your business services, pricing, hours, and booking information
  • By using our service, you (the business owner) authorize StyleDesk to send text messages on your behalf to customers who contact your business number
  • We do not send unsolicited marketing texts to customers unless you explicitly initiate a broadcast through the platform
  • Customers can stop receiving texts at any time by replying STOP to your business number
  • Standard message and data rates may apply to customers based on their carrier plan

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Encryption of data at rest in our databases
  • Row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure authentication and session management
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Limited employee access to production data on a need-to-know basis

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you promptly in the event of a data breach that affects your personal information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

7.1 Access and Portability

You can access your call history, text conversations, and account information through your dashboard at any time. You may also request a copy of your data by contacting us.

7.2 Correction

You can update your business information, services, pricing, and hours at any time through your dashboard settings.

7.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at hello@styledesk.ai. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or resolving disputes).

7.4 Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, your account will remain accessible until the end of your current billing period. After that, your data will be retained for 90 days before permanent deletion, giving you time to reactivate if needed.

7.5 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email. Transactional emails (receipts, security alerts, service updates) cannot be opted out of while your account is active.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: All data is retained for the duration of your active subscription
  • Cancelled accounts: Data is retained for 90 days after the end of your billing period, then permanently deleted
  • Call recordings: Retained for 12 months from the date of the call, then automatically deleted
  • Text message history: Retained for the duration of your active subscription plus 90 days after cancellation
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations
  • Deletion requests: Processed within 30 days. Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 additional days before full purge

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You may request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. This right does not apply.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: You may limit our use of sensitive personal information to what is necessary to provide the service

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@styledesk.ai. We will verify your identity before processing your request and respond within 45 days.

10. Other State Privacy Rights

Residents of Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), and other states with consumer privacy laws may have similar rights to access, delete, and correct their data, as well as the right to opt out of targeted advertising and profiling.
