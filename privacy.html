Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

StyleDesk AI, LLC ("StyleDesk," "we," "us," or "our") operates StyleDesk.ai and provides AI-powered communication tools for barbers and nail technicians. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using StyleDesk.ai, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number when you sign up
  • Your shop name, business type, and booking link
  • Services, pricing, and business hours you enter in your dashboard
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe — we do not store card details)

Information collected automatically:

  • Inbound call logs including caller phone numbers, call duration, and call status
  • Inbound and outbound SMS messages between your business number and your clients
  • Topics discussed during calls and texts (e.g., pricing, hours, booking)
  • Whether a booking link was sent during a conversation
  • Usage data such as login activity and dashboard interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provision and manage your dedicated business phone number
  • Power the AI voice and SMS responses on your behalf
  • Send booking links and automated messages to your clients
  • Display your call and text history in your dashboard
  • Send you weekly and monthly reports (Pro and Premium plans)
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Twilio — to power phone number provisioning, voice calls, and SMS delivery
  • OpenAI — to generate AI responses during calls and text conversations
  • Supabase — to store your account and conversation data securely
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments
  • Law enforcement or legal process — when required by law or to protect our rights

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it to provide services to us.

4. Your Clients' Data

When your clients call or text your StyleDesk business number, we collect their phone numbers and the content of their messages in order to respond on your behalf and log the conversation in your dashboard.

As a StyleDesk customer, you are responsible for ensuring your clients are aware that their communications may be handled by an AI assistant. We recommend noting this on your booking page or business listings.

We do not use your clients' data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active. Call logs and SMS conversations are retained for up to 12 months and may be deleted upon request.

If you cancel your subscription, your data will be retained for 30 days before permanent deletion, giving you time to export any records you need.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard measures including:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-level security on our database
  • Restricted access to production systems
  • Secure API key management

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Export your call and text history from your dashboard

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@styledesk.ai.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain dashboard functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

StyleDesk.ai is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify active subscribers by email for material changes. Your continued use of StyleDesk.ai after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
